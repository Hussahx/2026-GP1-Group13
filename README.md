# Rasid – Eye of the Desert
### AI-Powered Drone-Based Search & Rescue System

---

## Abstract

**Rasid (Eye of the Desert)** is a smart and integrated web-based system designed to enhance search and rescue operations in desert areas.  
It combines drone surveillance, AI-based human detection, geolocation tracking, and a comprehensive web platform for managing reports, volunteers, and operations.

The main goal is to reduce search time, improve detection accuracy, and support rescue teams efficiently.

---

## Problem Background

Search and rescue in desert regions face several challenges:

- Vast geographic areas  
- Harsh environmental conditions  
- Limited visual landmarks  
- Slow manual detection processes  

Traditional methods rely on human observation, which increases time, cost, and risk. Rasid provides an automated, AI-powered solution.

---

## Proposed Solution

Rasid offers:

- Live aerial video data collection via drones  
- AI-powered human detection  
- Real-time geolocation tracking  
- Integrated web platform for managing reports, volunteers, operation history, and team membership  

The system analyzes drone video frame by frame, detects human presence in real time, and records coordinates for efficient tracking.

---

## System Workflow

1. Drones begin area surveillance.  
2. Live video feed is transmitted to the processing unit.  
3. AI analyzes each frame for human detection.  
4. Detected coordinates are recorded.  
5. Reports are managed through the web platform.  
6. All operations are logged in the system history.

---

## Ethical & Operational Considerations

- Human verification ensures report accuracy.  
- Reports are not automatically sent to authorities without review.  
- Designed to support, not replace, rescue teams.
